FAQ

What is Growdy?

A slither-style play-to-earn game on Solana. Grow your worm, earn dickoin, convert to $GROWDY, and compete for season rewards.

Is it free to play?

Yes! No upfront cost. Just open the site and start playing — your burner wallet is created automatically.

Do I need a wallet to play?

No external wallet required. A burner wallet is auto-generated and saved in your browser. Back up your secret key from the Settings tab in-game (or the wallet button in the header) to import into Phantom or Solflare.

What is dickoin?

Dickoin is the in-game currency you earn passively from Phase 2 onward. At Phase 2 you unlock the swap to convert dickoin → $GROWDY in the P2E panel — but watch out for taxes!

How do I earn $GROWDY?

Earn dickoin passively, convert it in the P2E panel, win tenders, and reach Phase 6 to qualify for the Season Exit Pool.

What is Grow Gacha?

Spend $GROWDY in the Gacha tab (Phase 2+). ~55% of pulls are zonk (nothing or 1–2 dickoin dust). Good rewards are extremely rare — legendary is ~0.002%. Each pull costs 50 $GROWDY.

How do Guild Raids work?

Raid windows open on a schedule. Join a guild, lock a $GROWDY entry fee, and grind during the window — your guild score is total dickoin earned, phase reached, and clicks. The top guild splits the prize pool by each member's contribution. Upgrade the Guild Raids skill and Raid Squad worker to survive burn longer during raids.

Is this pay-to-win?

No. Progress comes from play time and strategy. Holding $GROWDY unlocks phases and boosts — but skill and consistency still win raids.

What happens if I go impotent?

Your IMPOTENT bar resets and you drop one phase. Use LIQUIDATE ASSETS to convert dickoin → IMPOTENT bar at 1:1 if you're in a pinch.

How do I buy $GROWDY?

Head to the Token page and hit "Buy on Pump.fun", or copy the contract address and swap on any supported DEX.

Can I use my own wallet?

Yes. Open the Settings tab in-game (or the wallet button in the header) → Export Secret Key, then import into Phantom or Solflare to take full control.
