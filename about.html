Guild Raids

  • Schedule: Raid windows open on a fixed timer (e.g. 48 hours). Only progress during the window counts.
  • Entry: Join a guild and lock a $GROWDY fee when the raid starts. Every entry fee goes into one prize pool.
  • Scoring: Guild score = sum of all members' dickoin earned, highest phase reached, and clicks — measured only inside the raid window.
  • Winner: The guild with the highest total score when the window closes takes the pool.
  • Payout: Winning guild members split the pool by contribution — if you contributed 20% of your guild's score, you get 20% of the pool. Non-winners forfeit their entry fee.
